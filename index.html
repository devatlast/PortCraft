About PortCraft

PortCraft is a multi-stage web application designed to demonstrate the power of interactive UI. It challenges users with a classic game of Rock Paper Scissors to unlock a hidden "persuasive" interface, eventually revealing a high-performance Etch A Sketch style grid that functions as a professional portfolio builder.
